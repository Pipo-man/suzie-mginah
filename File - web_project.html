GENERAL VIEW OF 6th EASTERN AFRICA STATISTICAL TRAINING CENTRE CONVOCATION CEREMONY.


Over view of 6th EASTC convocation ceremony . As usual our tradition of gathering as single family of academicians, experts in professionals, students and benefactors as stakeholders of university community discussing and exchanging views leading to impact the research and professional development.
This year the topic selected is 'the role of statistics and big data in economic transformation and sustainable development of Tanzania.'


Main theme of the convocation is as follows

modernising data ecosystems to accelerate implementation of the African continental free trade area: the role of statistics and big data in development and Sustainability of Africa
